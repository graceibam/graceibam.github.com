Grace Bambushew



Grace was born in Princeton, New Jersey to her parents Stuart and Valerie. When she was one year old, she and her family moved to Stratham, New Hampshire, where she grew up with her two brothers, Shawn and Brian. After graduating Exeter High School in Exeter, New Hampshire, Grace went on to study Mechanical Engineering at Tufts University.

In college, Grace played club sports and worked as a fellow at Tufts' Center for Engineering Education Outreach in their Student Teacher Outreach Mentorship Program.

After Graduation, Grace plans to join the Navy as a Nuclear Power Officer on aircraft carriers.
